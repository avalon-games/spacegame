Player Movement Controls:


Basic: 
a / d - left and right movement
wasd - movement on ship

space - jump

left click - hold to launch a grapple to pull yourself towards the attached point, release button to release grapple

right click - hold to launch a grapple to swing yourself around the attached point, release button to release grapple

left shift - hold to enter slowMo mode, playing the game at 0.4 speed, allows player to specify the grapple direction more easily while moving, release button to exit

f - launch a bullet that will activate pressure plates 

esc - open menu

` - toggle open debug console, use 'help' to show the list of available commands
